## What's Changed
Initial release of NappyQuit

Features:
* Record potty activities: Tried the potty, Used the potty, Accident
* Track water intake with "some water" or "lots of water" amounts
* Log food consumption times
* Track nappy changes
* Detailed activity logging: What happened (pee, poo, both) and how (told parents, went by themselves, asked to sit)
* Quick tap records current time, long-press to select specific date/time
* Edit and delete existing log entries
* View complete activity history
* Multilingual support: English and Hungarian
* Privacy-focused: All data stored locally on device
* Clean, emoji-based interface for easy navigation
