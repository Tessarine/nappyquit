NappyQuit is a simple and intuitive potty training assistant app designed to help parents track their child's potty training journey.

Features:
• Record potty activities: Track when your child tried the potty, successfully used it, or had an accident
• Log water intake: Monitor hydration levels with "some water" or "lots of water" options
• Track food consumption: Record meal times to identify patterns
• Nappy changes: Keep track of nappy changes and progress
• Activity details: Note what happened (pee, poo, or both) and how it happened (child told parents, went by themselves, or asked to sit)
• Time flexibility: Quick tap to record current time, or long-press to select a specific date and time
• Edit and delete: Full control over your logged entries
• Log history: View and manage all recorded activities with timestamps
• Multilingual support: Available in multiple languages including English and Hungarian
• Privacy-focused: All data stays on your device - no cloud storage or account required
• Clean interface: Simple, emoji-based buttons for easy navigation

Perfect for parents who want to:
- Track potty training progress over time
- Identify patterns in their child's bathroom habits
- Maintain a consistent training routine
- Share progress with caregivers or pediatricians

The app is completely free, open-source, and contains no ads or tracking. Your child's data stays private on your device.
